---
Title: "The RemCo Contract"
Order: 2
---

Remilia Collective artists commit to exclusively selling NFT art on the Remilia Collection market, providing 30% flat commission to Remilia Corp and 10% additional into Remilia Co-op funding (60% final compensation) on every sale.

Remilia Corp will match 10% funding into the Remilia Co-op (effective Co-op contribution = 20% total of all NFT sales), with other 20% retained for Remilia Corp's working capital for operations, compensation etc.

Official arrangement is ownership of work is transferred to Remilia Corp on consignment when it is delivered to the Collection and the NFT is minted, with payment distributed back to the artist from Remilia Corp as a portion of the sale when eventually sold.
