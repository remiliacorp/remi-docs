Self-hosted, semi-public digital infrastructure providing community services to Remilia Collective and Remilia friends & family. The importance of Remilia-hosted services will become more evident as global instability increasingly descends onto our world and threatens our basic digital infrastructure.

# Social:

## Remilia Chat - Asynchrous IRC
Slack-like Zulip with mailing-list style conversation threading.

## Remilia.wired - Unstructured real-time IRC. 
Realtime IRC where each letter typed is instantly visible to all other users. This app is being built by our sister collective, XCELA, on commission for Remilia.

# Media:

## Remilia Radio - Algorithm Web Radio
Web radio algorithm designed in a 24 hour schedule of 2-hour blocks organized by niche "genre" playlists played on an album-by-album shuffle. All albums shared into the radio station is available for public on-demand streaming on an FTP interface.

### Remilia Music - Shared Music Library
A collaborative music library where users can all upload their music and stream it freely. Accomplished through "Funkwhale", a federated application. Only  Remilia Collective members will have rights to upload music.

## Remilia Bookshelf - Shared PDF Library
A curated selection of PDFs for view/download. Accomplished through Calibre-web.

## Remilia Archive - Web Archive
Self-hosted wayback machine, via archivematica
