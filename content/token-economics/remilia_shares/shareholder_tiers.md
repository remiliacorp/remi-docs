Benefits are allotted to Shareholders, categorized into Shareholder tiers. Shareholder tiers are determined by the number of Remilia Shares held in a single wallet.

## Rights

**|           Share Tier            | Onyx | Quartz | Emerald | Jade | Imperial |**
|:-------------------------------:|:----:|:------:|:-------:|:----:|:--------:|
**|         Shares Required         |   1  |    5   |    10   |  20  |    100   |**
|       Patronage Fund Votes      |   1  |    5   |    15   |  25  |    150   |
| Standard Grade NFT Drop Tickets |   1  |    5   |    10   |  20  |    100   |
|  Emerald Grade NFT Drop Tickets |   0  |    1   |    3    |   7  |    40    |
|  Jadeite Grade NFT Drop Tickets |   0  |    0   |    0    |   1  |     5    |

**|    Share Tier    | Onyx | Quartz | Emerald | Jade | Imperial |**

|                  |      |        |         |   X  |     X    |

Onyx - 1 Share: 
- **One (1) Ticket for Standard Art Drops**
- **One (1) Vote in the Patronage Fund**
- Access to Remilia Chat

Quartz - 5 Shares: 
- **Five (5) Tickets for Standard Art Drops**
- **Five (5) Votes in the Patronage Fund**
- **One (1) Ticket for Emerald NFT drops**
- *Access to Remilia Chat*
- Access to Private Remilia Gallery

Emerald - 10 Shares:
- **Ten (10) Tickets for Standard Art Drops**
- **Ten (10) Votes in the Patronage Fund**
- **Two (2) Tickets for Emerald NFT drops**
- *Access to Remilia Chat*
- *Access to Private Remilia Gallery*
- Access to Private Art Auctions
- Early access to Project Development

Jade - 20 Shares:
- **Twenty (20) Ticket sfor Standard Art Drops**
- **Five (5) Votes in the Patronage Fund**
- **Three (3) Ticket sfor Emerald NFT drops**
- **One (1) Ticket for Jadeite Grade Art drops
- *Access to Remilia Chat*
- *Access to Private Remilia Gallery*
- *Access to Private Art Auctions*
- *Early access to Project Development*
