# Technical Specifications

## Engine

Remilia Gallery will be built in Unreal Engine as it supports architectural render grade light engine, essential for duplicating the experience of real-world art viewing in a gallery.

Unreal Engine also has built-in and well optimized support for Virtual Reality equipment, as well as multiplayer servers including VoIP.

## Payment Integration

Ability to purchase art and exchange ownership of NFT Art directly within the gallery can be achieve through MetaMask, communicating to OpenSea through its SDK.

## Installment Specifications

For the sake of curator ease of use and due to the novelty of the VR Gallery medium, NFT art will be restricted to easily presented mediums:
- 2D Image/Gif
- 3D Sculpture/Installation
- Video projection
